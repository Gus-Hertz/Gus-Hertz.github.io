Adventure Hooks

I love the song at the beginning of this video, and I play it at the start of my games. You don't have to play this by any means to start your game, but I like it. Song finishes at 3:52, and goes into player/character introductions.

Choose an adventure hook that fits with your group.

  1. Given a job by Lord Latida, "La-Ti-Da", (or pick an existing NPC’s in your game). Disappearance of the Lord Latida's son Dasbad. Dasbad has been kidnapped. The assailants grabbed him on his way home from the pub. Pub owner saw the assailants dragging the boy off in a sack in to the forest. Players who ask motive for taking the boy. Diplomacy dc 10= most likely ransom. DC15 Although Lord Latida is highly respected Dasbad has a bad reputation of making many problems for the towns various pub's. Latida uses his status to keep his son out of jail.
    • Works almost all the time because players know they will be paid.
  2. ⦁ Players witness the sons kidnapping and immediately go after the assailants.
    • Works well with a party that has a strong sense of justice.
  3. Players wander across the tower randomly while walking through an enchanted forest. Could force players in with bad weather.
    • Works well when the group has someone who is an instigator, or is just curious of old ruins.

Upon reaching the Tower the party will attempt to rescue Dasbad from his captors the Kenku.
