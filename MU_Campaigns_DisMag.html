Discovering Magic

Discovering Magic is a campaign that takes place in a low magic setting. The parties goal is to find and defeat the Council of Bael Turith. The Council is a group humans who wanted magic so badly they made Infernal pacts and became warlocks. To test their new found power they attack a neighboring nataion of Eladrin and kill the royal family. One surviving Prince contacts the adventurers to bring down the Council of Bael Turith.

After killing the royal family the Council of Bael Turith has gone into hiding to learn more about their new powers. Council Members are spread across the world living in major large cities where their activities are overlooked. Finding them is a hard task let alone trying to fight them. Each council member has its own cult of followers that will do everything in their power to stop the players.

Since the Council is such a large organization they can be the main villian that your group hunts down. Alternativly they can be an recurring side villain that tries to foil the players plans whenever they are in a large city.
