Adventure Wrap Up 

Depending on what hook you use, and outcome of the Adventure.

  1. Players find Dasbad, save him, and escort him home. Reward- 25 gold. 2 potions of healing.
  2. Players find Dasbad dead but return body to his father. They will know its Dasbad based on his heraldy swen on clothes. Reward- 12 gold 1 potion of healing.
  3. Players find Dasbad dead but don't return the dead body to father. Reward- 10 gold.
  4. Players don't save him and can't figure out who he is or never get the chance to figure it out. (Possible to happen with hook 3) Reward- none
