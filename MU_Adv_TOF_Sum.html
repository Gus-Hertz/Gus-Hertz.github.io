Tower of Feathers

Tower of Feathers is an adventure designed for use with fifth edition D&D materials. Intended for a group of 4-5 1st level characters. During the adventure, the party attempts to rescue Dasbad, a nobles' eldest son, from the fowl clutches of the Kenku. Dasbad has ruffled some feathers and wound up kidnapped. The party's goal is to find Dasbad and rescue him from his captors.

General flow of the game.

  1. Introduce plot with an adventure hook to get the party to rescue Dasbad.
  2. Rescue Dasbad from the villian's lair.
  3. Wrapping up the adventure (Reward).
